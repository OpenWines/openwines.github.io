Open Data for Wines

What is OpenWines?

Our primary goal is to gather and share datasets of genuine open-data licensed resources about wines.

Our secondary goal is to deliver nice data visualizations and mapping about wines, in various regions of the world.

Work in progress!

Contact: hello@openwines.eu

Are you a Wine Startup?

No, we are a non-profit organization.

We started OpenWines in March, 2015: OpenWines is not a startup or a commercial initiative: we are building an open-data collaborative project about wines, vineyars & winegrowers.

'Open' means: free, available, reusable, redistribuable, interoperable, and open to contributions. Not only the data are free, but also the tools, softwares, schemas, design, illustration and documentation that come with them. This will remain as is, for ever, thanks to an open-data license.

We need data,
and we will share them!

We need data, with your genuine permissions to share.

Send your materials (csv, xls., json, etc) to:

data@openwines.eu

Any raw data is accepted. All shared data will be reditributed here with a "Share Alike" agreement: We are thinking about the opportunity of the Open Database License.
